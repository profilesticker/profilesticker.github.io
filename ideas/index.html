Do you have an awesome idea?


Every idea is awesome! Do you have one that could possibly make Profile Sticker a better web app? If yes, please feel free to suggest them to us. Here is how you can do that -

Create a free GitHub account, if you don't have one already. Next, visit Profile Sticker's issue and idea tracking page on GitHub. Please click on the "New issue" button on the top right of the screen. Don't worry if the button says 'new issue' instead of 'new idea'. We're using this as a more generic way of capturing ideas and issues.



Once you've clicked the button as showed in the pictue above, you should see a screen like the picture below. Fill in a suitable title for your new idea and give as much details as possible in the description area. Also, do not hesitate to attach any mock-ups/screenshots/documents, etc. Once you've filled up the required details, click on "Submit new issue". Upon hitting that button, your idea will be submitted to the portal and we will review it shortly.
