So, why should you contribute?

Apart from this being an incredibly creative opportunity, here is why you should definitely start contributing -


Start creating your online footprint. Get recognised for the stickers you design.

Stand out from the rest of your friends and watch them and many others use your sticker on their profile pictures.

Add a practical experience to your CV.

Give back to the community and the open-source space.


What is Profile Sticker?

Profile Sticker is a web app that lets you add stickers from different themes on your profile picture.

Try it now Learn more


And did you ask how must I contribute?

Glad you did. Simply send us the following -


Your full name that you'd like to see in the credits against the stickers you design.

Your online website/portfolio link that we can link back to.

Two PNG images of the sticker you created - one of 512px by 512px and the other of 1024px by 1024px.

A suitable title for the sticker, the category for which you designed the sticker for and other details such as any copyright links, etc.
