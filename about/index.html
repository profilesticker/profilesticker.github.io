About Profile Sticker

A little background on Profile Sticker

Profile Sticker is all about adding some fun and awesome stickers to your profile picture making your profile picture stand out from the rest of the crowd. Profile Sticker launched on the 1st of July 2017. It was conceptualized as a part of another project but was later separated out as an open-source product.

Apart from providing some trendy and cool stickers for users to be able to paste onto their profile picture, the wider goal was to be able to create a product that not only other developers from the industry could contribute but also make it easier for a designer to showcase their skills and designs on the site. More information on that is available on this publicly available wiki page.

Adding stickers must be difficult, isn't it?

Nah, not at all. Its the most easiest thing you'd have to do. You start by uploading a profile picture from your computer or your profile picture from Facebook. You can then crop the picture to a perfect square. Once that's done, click next and you will then be able to choose a sticker from a wide range of designs. *After a long pause* You liked a sticker? Well, that's great news. You may now apply that sticker to your profile picture and click next. This screen will now allow you to download the edited image or you may post the picture directly to your Facebook timeline.
