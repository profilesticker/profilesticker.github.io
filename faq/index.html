FAQ

What is Profile Sticker exactly?

Profile Sticker is all about adding some cool stickers to your profile picture making your profile picture stand out from the rest of the crowd. Profile Sticker has different sticker from various themese/categories. With 100 stickers to choose from, Profile Sticker launched on the 1st of July 2017.

I love the stickers on Profile Sticker. Can I contribute my sticker too?

Of course, you can. We'd really appreciate your contribution to Profile Sticker. Please visit our how to contribute page and follow the instructions outlined there to submit your design. Each design will have a link back to the designer's online portfolio/profile.

I am unable to connect to Facebook while uploading a picture and/or posting the image to Facebook. Why does that happen?

Here are a couple of things you could check to ensure that you're able to connect to Facebook without any hassle.
  • Ensure that any popup blockers are not blocking the Facebook login screen from showing up. You may find one at the top right of your browser if its enabled.
  • When you do login to Facebook, please do not forget to authorize the app when asked for in the next screen. If you do not do this, you may not be able to post the picture.

Does Profile Sticker save my Facebook details?

Absolutely not. When you login to Facebook, we do not save your Facebook details. Your profile picture is also not saved anywhere on our servers.

What does the toggle button in the sticker preview area of step 3 do?

The toggle button is for you to be able to view your picture with and without the sticker. That toggle button in the preview sticker area doesn't actually apply the sticker.

Why can't I crop my picture to a free-size form?

At this stage, we're able to only process a square image and thus, during the crop step we only allow only a square (1:1 ratio) selection.
