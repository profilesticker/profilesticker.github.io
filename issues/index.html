Did you spot an issue with Profile Sticker?


Well, it's fair to admit that as much of an effort we are putting towards developing this web app and making sure it works at its finest, there is always a possibility that there could be something that has missed our eyes. Therefore, every issue that you raise would help in improving the quality of Profile Sticker and if you do spot an issue, please feel free to raise it with us. Here is how you can do that -

Create a free GitHub account, if you don't have one already. Next, visit Profile Sticker's issue and idea tracking page on GitHub. Please click on the "New issue" button on the top right of the screen.



Once you've clicked the button as showed in the pictue above, you should see a screen like the picture below. Fill in a suitable title for your new idea and give as much details as possible in the description area. Also, do not hesitate to attach any mock-ups/screenshots/documents, etc. Once you've filled up the required details, click on "Submit new issue".
